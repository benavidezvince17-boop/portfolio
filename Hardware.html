PROJECT: CARDUINO

PROJECT TYPE: ARDUINO
OBJECTIVE: Build and Program a car robot using arduino
DATE CREATED: January 15, 2024

PROJECT CONTENT:


Project Summary

A Carduino is a small car that uses an Arduino board to move and perform tasks. It can go forward, backward, and turn, and can also avoid obstacles or follow lines using sensors. Carduino projects are often used by students to learn about electronics, programming, and robotics. They can be controlled by Bluetooth, Wi-Fi, or made to drive automatically. In short, it‚Äôs a simple and fun way to understand how real smart cars and robots work.


Uses and Applications:

  • Learning tool for students to understand electronics, programming, and robotics.
  • Obstacle-avoidance robot that can detect and avoid objects automatically.
  • Line-following robot for path navigation and automation experiments.
  • Bluetooth or Wi-Fi controlled car for wireless control using smartphones.
  • Prototype for self-driving or delivery systems in small environments.
  • STEM education project used in schools and research demonstrations.
  • Testing platform for sensors, motors, and control systems.
